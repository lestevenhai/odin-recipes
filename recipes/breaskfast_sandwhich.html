Breakfast Sandwhich

Make this quick and simple breakfast sandwich in the morning for your family or just yourself. The ingredients are easy to find and put together.

Ingredients

  • 1 English muffin - buttermilk (optimal)
  • 1 Slice of american cheese
  • 1 Bunsize pork or turkey breakfast sausage
  • 2 Eggs
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Put english muffin in airfyer at 400 degrees for 5 minutes
  2. After 5 minutes put the slice of cheese on one side of the egnlish muffin
  3. Whisk eggs in a bowl and then heat up pan
  4. Cook eggs in pan and shape into a circle or use a egg ring
  5. Heat up sausage patty
  6. Assemble sandwhich and enjoy
